LEAN SESSIONS

What it means

Lean Sessions is a structured, agenda-less meeting where participants democratically build the itinerary and time-box discussions.

The format

How it works

01

Brainstorming

Start with the topics in the room.

Participants write down the topics they want to discuss on sticky notes or a whiteboard.

02

Voting

Let the group pull the agenda into focus.

Everyone is given a set number of votes, usually three, to place on the topics they care about most. The facilitator organizes the board with the most-voted topics at the top.

03

Discussion

Talk, time-box, then decide what is worth more time.

The group discusses the top-voted topic first, and conversation is strictly time-boxed, typically five to ten minutes. When the timer goes off, the group votes on whether to continue discussing the current topic or move to the next one on the agenda.
