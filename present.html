Lean Coffee Session

Where It Started

Seattle, 2009

Lean Coffee was created by Jim Benson and Jeremy Lightsmith as a lightweight way to bring people together without building a heavy meeting structure first.

The idea was simple: let the people in the room build the agenda, vote on what matters, and keep each conversation time-boxed.

The Concept

Structured, agenda-less

Participants propose the topics, vote on priorities, and discuss the highest-value items first.

The facilitator protects the flow: focused discussion, visible topics, clear timing, and democratic decision-making.
